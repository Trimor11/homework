Why use an AI math problem solver?

Struggling with a tricky algebra equation or a geometry proof? An AI math solver doesn't just give you the answer — it shows you every step so you learn the method, not just copy the result. Whether you're in middle school or college, our tool adapts to your level and explains in plain English.

What types of math can I solve?

Our AI handles all major math topics. Algebra — solve equations, factor polynomials, inequalities and systems. Geometry — area, volume, angles, proofs, and coordinate geometry. Calculus — derivatives, integrals, limits, and differential equations. Statistics — mean, median, probability, and distributions. Arithmetic — fractions, percentages, ratios, and word problems.

How to get the best math answers

Type your problem exactly as it appears — you can use standard notation like "2x + 5 = 15" or describe it in words. For best results, be specific. Include the subject if it's ambiguous, e.g. "statistics: find the mean of 4, 7, 9, 12". If the first answer is too complex, hit 🔁 Simpler for a more basic explanation.
