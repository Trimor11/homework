A real example

Question: Solve for x: 3x + 7 = 22
Step 1: Identify the goal — we need to get x alone on one side of the equation.
Step 2: Subtract 7 from both sides: 3x + 7 − 7 = 22 − 7, which gives us 3x = 15.
Step 3: Divide both sides by 3: 3x ÷ 3 = 15 ÷ 3, which gives us x = 5.
Summary: x = 5. We isolated x by subtracting first, then dividing.
