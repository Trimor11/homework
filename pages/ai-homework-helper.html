What is an AI homework helper?

An AI homework helper is like having a private tutor available any time. You ask your homework question, and the AI explains the answer in clear, numbered steps — not just a final answer. This helps you understand the why behind every solution so you can tackle similar questions on your own.

Every subject, one tool

Our AI detects your subject automatically and adjusts its explanation style. 📐 Math — algebra, geometry, calculus, statistics, trigonometry. ⚗️ Science — biology, chemistry, physics, earth science. 📜 History — world history, U.S. history, civics, social studies. 📚 English — grammar, essay writing, literary analysis, vocabulary. 🌍 Geography — countries, capitals, climate, physical geography.

How is it different from Googling?

When you Google a homework question you get a list of links — some relevant, some not, all requiring you to dig through ads and other pages. SolverPro gives you one direct, personalized, step-by-step answer in seconds. No links, no digging, no ads in the answer.

Tips for the best answers

Be specific — "Solve 3x + 7 = 22" works better than "solve this equation". Add context if helpful — "explain photosynthesis for a 7th grade science class". If the answer feels too complex, click 🔁 Explain Simpler and the AI will regenerate with easier vocabulary and analogies. You can also export any answer to PDF for offline studying.
